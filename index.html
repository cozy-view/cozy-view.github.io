Features

Built for the way you actually browse.

Less clutter. Fewer taps. Just the sites and videos you come back to.

Minimal site home

Pin your most-visited sites to a clean tile grid. Tap once, you're there — no bookmarks folder to dig through.

Mini player (PiP)

Keep watching while you scroll. Videos slide into a floating mini player so you never lose your place.

Background play

Lock the screen, switch apps — your audio keeps going. Perfect for music, podcasts, and long videos.

Bookmarks & history

Star a page to save it. Browse what you've visited. Swipe to remove entries one by one, or clear everything at once.

Smart search

Autocomplete suggestions pulled from your history and common terms. Find what you need in fewer keystrokes.

Dark mode & multi-language

Follows your device's system theme and language automatically. Comfortable to use from morning coffee to midnight scroll.

FAQ

Quick answers.

More questions? See the full support page.

How do I open a site?
Tap any tile on the home screen — the site opens in the built-in browser. You can also use the search bar to type a URL or search query.
When does the mini player activate?
When you leave a video screen on supported sites, the player slides into a floating mini window. You can move it around or tap to return to full screen.
Does the app collect my browsing data?
No. Bookmarks, history, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We use Google AdMob and Firebase for analytics and crash reporting — see the Privacy Policy for details.
Do I need an account?
No account needed. Open the app and start browsing.
What platforms is CozyView on?
iOS and Android. We recommend keeping your device on one of the latest two major OS versions for the best experience.
